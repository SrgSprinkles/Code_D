Our Mission Statement

To inform, instil passion, and create a culture in information security.

Who We Are

Cyber@UC is the official student led Information Security OWASP Organization for the University of Cincinnati. We try to spread the knowledge and importance of Information Security across all ages by doing outreaches or by collaboration with companies and other organizations.

About Us

Outreach

Travel out to local high schools to generate interest and teach about the importance of information security.

Research

Research bleeding-edge technologies like predictive intrusion detection systems and contribute to open source projects like cuckoo malware sandbox.

Privacy

Stress the importance of privacy by setting up fun and informative exercises to educate our members how hackers steal private information.

Collaborate

Connect with businesses and local organizations to offer our members unique opportunities to network within the field of cyber security.
